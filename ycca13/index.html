Specialisterne

Scroll to see the project

This project was made for the for the Danish YCCA 2013 together with Bo Mouridsen

The assignment was to encourage Danish companies to recruit more people with autism, aka “Specialists” and show them the special skills people with autism can add to the company.

Our idea was to analyze the company and find spots in the company where the specialists can make a difference and strengthen the company.

We made a web app where the company connects via Linkedin and finds all the employees from the company or a given team. The app analyzes the employees' skills and finds places in the team where a Specialist can be used. The app recommends the best qualified Specialists in the database from their skills, age and location.
