My name is Kasper Andersen, I'm a 23 year old Danish Digitized Graphic Designer. I'm currently working at the Aalborg based agency Det nye sort as a Junior Designer. I finished my BA at the School of visual communication in 2012 where I also took an internship at the Dutch agency Silo Design. Before I joined Det nye sort I worked for 2 months freelancing at the London based agency The Late Night Salon. You can see the rest of my resume and connect with me on Linkedin.

You should follow me on Twitter or see what music i like on Spotify. If you want to see some of my smaller projects and work-in-progress you should follow me on dribbble. If you want to talk or drink coffee with me, you should send me an E-mail.
